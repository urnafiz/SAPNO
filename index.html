What is SAPNO?

Social Advancement Program and Networking Organization (SAPNO) is a government recognized NGO whose programs are based on voluntary services and social development. SAPNO is well ahead as a non-political, non-profit organization. SAPNO has been involved in the field of development since 1998 as a non-governmental development organization in the northern part of Bangladesh. According to the current position of Bangladesh pertaining to the eradication of poverty and improvement of the socio-economic condition of abandoned, poor, landless, and the downtrodden, the need is to improve the health of the general people, by creating a clear and nice atmosphere free from air and water pollution. To keep all these in view, a handful of dedicated social workers after mutual consultation decided to set up an organization for the purpose Of ‘SAPNO’ thus came into existence in 1998. SAPNO’s main targets are to create self-employment, to keep the atmosphere free from pollution, to help helpless and needy persons, etc. One of those founder members, Md. Ziaur Rahman is now the Executive Director of SAPNO.
